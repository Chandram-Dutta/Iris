## Controls

- **Arrow Keys** or **WASD**: Move the snake
- **Space**: Restart after game over

## Gameplay

- Eat food to grow and score points
- Avoid hitting walls
- Avoid hitting yourself
- Speed increases every 5 foods eaten
- Normal food: 10 points (red)
- Bonus food: 50 points (gold)

## Visual Design

- Gradient snake from bright cyan-green (head) to dark green (tail)
- Pulsing food with glow effects
- Dark gradient background
- Clean UI with score, high score, and speed display
